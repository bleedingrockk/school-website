Courses Offered at Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti

Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti provides a well-rounded education with a focus on nurturing young minds. Our school-level courses are designed to foster academic excellence and personal development.

Primary Education

Our primary education program lays the foundation for a lifelong love of learning. We prioritize fundamental skills and knowledge, ensuring a smooth transition into higher grades.

Middle School

The middle school curriculum at Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti is crafted to provide a well-balanced education. Students explore a range of subjects, including mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies.

High School

In our high school program, students delve deeper into subject areas, preparing for future academic challenges. The curriculum is enriched with co-curricular activities to promote holistic development.
